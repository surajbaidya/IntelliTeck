About

Intellitek is a professional staffing and solutions firm that specialises in IT, Government, Insurance and Financial Services, Healthcare, Pharmaceuticals, and Clinical Research. We provide temporary, temporary-to-hire, and direct placement services for individuals and full teams as part of our staffing offering. Our commitment to making quality placements is built on our intimate knowledge of our customers’ local markets, businesses, IT initiatives, and company cultures. Our global reach, referral-based sourcing strategy, and rigorous screening process allow us to separate the best candidates from the rest of the pack. We make every effort to ensure that the professionals we send have the specific skills that our clients require in order to achieve their objectives. Our consultants have connections to the best IT jobs at the most prestigious businesses.

Goals

Intellitek’s mission is to provide our clients with world-class technology solutions. Intellitek is committed to providing our clients with exceptional, experienced individuals at a reasonable cost. Our focus on building strong and lasting relationships with our clients sets Intellitek apart from our competitors. We believe that success is achieved through collaboration, in which creative, innovative, and diverse individuals collaborate to make the workplace vibrant and exciting. Our employees are our most valuable asset, and their contributions make a difference. Individual perspectives are valued because they enable us to see in all directions with an open mind, which opens new doors. Our distinguishing feature is our team, which brings something special to our search for the next big business idea. VForce Infotech goes above and beyond. We are everything but ordinary.
